Quality

Specifies the encoding quality of the image. Since it's value is percentage, it must be in the interval of 0 - 100. (If undefined, defaults to 90%). If quality is less than 100% on jpeg and webp formats there is lossy compression, And on png, it is compression rate since there is no lossy compression with png. Quality can be lowered if more economic images are needed.
