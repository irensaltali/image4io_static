What are credits?
Credits are way to measure and represent the usage. 1 Credit is equal to 1 GB of CDN usage, 1GB of storage or 1000 image transformations. Price, credit and alternative comparison calculator can be found here.
I use lots of data. Can you help me?
Image4io can handle vast majority of data in large amounts. For special inquieries, contact us.
